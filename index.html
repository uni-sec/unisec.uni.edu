UNISEC - UNI Cyber Security Organization

					$ whoami
				
UNISEC is to educate and build interest around the field of cyber security for students at UNI. The organization will discuss security topics, be aware of current cyber situations and role-play as either blue team or red team at cyber defense competitions, host cyber defense competition events at UNI, and participate in other various cyber competitions such as capture the flag, cyber anomalies, etc. This program shall be open to all students to promote teamwork and education.
